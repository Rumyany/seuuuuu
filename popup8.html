Banana Nut Pancakes

Banana Nut Pancakes are a delightful breakfast variation of classic pancakes, combining the sweet, creamy essence of ripe bananas with the satisfying crunch of chopped nuts. These pancakes feature mashed bananas and chopped nuts folded into the batter, creating a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Served hot and drizzled with maple syrup, they offer a perfect balance of fruity sweetness and nutty richness, making them a comforting breakfast treat.
