S'mores Pancakes

S'mores Pancakes are a mouthwatering breakfast creation inspired by the beloved campfire treat. Here's a description: S'mores Pancakes are a decadent breakfast delight that pays homage to the classic campfire treat. These pancakes are a fusion of fluffy pancake goodness and the irresistible combination of graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate. To craft S'mores Pancakes, the pancake batter is infused with graham cracker crumbs, imparting a subtle hint of graham cracker flavor and a pleasant, slightly crumbly texture. Miniature marshmallows and chocolate chips are then folded into the batter, ensuring that each bite is filled with gooey marshmallow goodness and melty chocolate decadence. As these pancakes sizzle on the griddle, the marshmallows gently toast, creating a delightful caramelized exterior that adds a delightful crunch to the overall experience. The aroma of toasting marshmallows and melting chocolate wafts through the kitchen, promising a truly indulgent breakfast.
