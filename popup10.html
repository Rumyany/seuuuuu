Blueberry Pancakes

Blueberry pancakes are a variation of classic pancakes with added fresh or frozen blueberries for flavor and color. Here's a simplified recipe: **Ingredients:** - 1 1/2 cups flour - 2 tbsp sugar - 2 tsp baking powder - 1/2 tsp salt - 1 1/4 cups milk - 1 egg - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 cup blueberries **Instructions:** 1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. 2. Beat egg, add milk and oil in another bowl. Combine with dry mix. 3. Cook pancakes with blueberries on a griddle. 4. Serve with syrup or toppings of choice. Enjoy these fruity, colorful pancakes!
